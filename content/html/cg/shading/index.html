Back Face 判斷

the inner product of N and (-V) is less than 0, the polygon is back face

(polygon with normal N and the viewing vector V from the eye point to the polygon)

Back Face Culling

perform in normalized coordinate ( or clip coordinate)

Perform

nornal vector N =(A, B, C)

viewing direction vector V(0, 0, -1) (orthogonal projection)

the dot product of N and V is C

if C < 0, the polygon is back face

else the polygon is front face

constant shading

compute the intensity of the polygon with face normal, and draw all pixels in the polygon with the intensity

Gouraud shading

use vertex intensity to interpolate the intensity of pixels over the polygon

Pixel’s intensity is obtained by bilinear interpolation of

vertex’s intensities

Produces incorrect highlights on a polygon

不能正確的產生出 highlights

Phong shading

interpolate the normal for interior pixels and compute the intensity at interior pixel

Phong shading cannot (because we cannot get the normal vectors of interior pixels in fixed pipeline)

Pixel’s normal is obtained by bilinear interpolation of

vertex’s normals

Can give more accurate specular effects

Phong shading is computationally more expensive

than Gouraud shading
